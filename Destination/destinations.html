Sikkim

A trip to the capital of Sikkim, Gangtok, is included in most Sikkim tour packages. The town enjoys pleasant climate throughout the year and is an extremely popular tourist destination.
Sikkim tour packages include visits to major tourist attractions in Gangtok like Enchey Monastery, Banjhakri Falls, Do-drul Chorten and Dodrupchen Monastery.
While in Gangtok, a visit to the Namgyal Institute of Tibet logy is a must. the traditional Thukpa or momos are a definite must-try while on a Sikkim tour.
